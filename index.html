TYPES OF KNOWLEDGE

1. Declaritive Knowledge

It consists of facts that can be stated verbally such as propositions about person,places,things and events

2. Procedural Knowledge

It is a type of knowledge which is responsible for deciding how to do things. It is also known as Imperitive Knowledge.

3. Meta Knowledge

Knowledge about other type of knowledge is reffered to as Meta Knowledge.

4. Heuristic Knowledge

It is a rule of thumb based on previous experiences,awareness of approaches and situation which are good to work but are not guaranteed.

5. Structural Knowledge

It is the basic knowledge to problem solving.It describes the relationship that exists between objects or concepts.
